Methodology applied for PC DIY

2019-04-21
#PC DIY #Computer #Methodology
Methodology

I am never a PC DIY expert, but I successfully assembled a gaming PC last month, completely DIY, purchasing CPU, graphics, RAM etc. and make them work together.

It is the methodology I want to share more than the PC stuff. I always follow this methodology when I did research, solve technical problems and so on. Let me share my theory to you.

Step 1: Get to know the key components of a computer

Firstly, We need to figure out which components are the most important ones in a computer and their performance can eventually determine the performance of a computer.

Although different people may have different concerns, we still could not deny CPU and graphic are the keys, which are like human’s brain and heart.

So we have our Step 1 conclusion:

a. CPU

b.graphic

######

Step 2: Determine which CPU and graphic to buy by demand

In this step, we could use a CPU ladder and a graphic ladder which could be acquired from Google easily.

Then I considered that I am a programmer, I use computer a lot, so I need powerful CPU, and I play games, so I need good graphic.

Besides, as the AI technology are thriving today, maybe some day I become interested in machine learning development, so a better GPU is good.
